What is Golf Tribe?

Golf Tribe is a modern-tailored golf simulator built in a shed. Why did we choose to go this route? We want to provide an opportunity for you to take advantage of the most advanced launch monitor technology at a fraction of the price of other hitting bays. Now you have the opportunity to use the same technology as the professionals to improve your game. Come join the Tribe.

What Are You Looking To Do?

Whether you're looking for a round of golf on a stormy day, or want to see what the state of the art technology can do to help improve your swing, our services have you covered.
